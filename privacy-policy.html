Privacy Policy for Resonote

Data Collection and Usage

Resonote is designed with user privacy in mind:

  • Audio Data: Audio captured from browser tabs is processed locally via our desktop application or sent to external AI services only when explicitly enabled by the user
  • Transcription Data: Transcripts are stored locally in your browser and can be exported or deleted at any time
  • Personal Communications: We process audio content from meetings, videos, and browser tabs solely for transcription purposes
  • Website Content: We access web page content only to detect video streams and audio sources for transcription

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share user data with third parties. Audio may be sent to external AI services only when users explicitly enable cloud-based transcription features.

Data Retention

Users have full control over their data. Transcription history is stored locally and can be cleared at any time through the extension interface.
