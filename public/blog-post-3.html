Resonote 2.0: A New Era of Intelligence

By The Team • Nov 01, 2025

We are thrilled to announce the release of Resonote 2.0, our biggest update since launch. We've rebuilt our core engine from the ground up to deliver faster, more accurate transcriptions and powerful new AI capabilities.

What's New?

Enhanced AI Analysis

Our new "AI Insights" tab gives you instant summaries, action item extraction, and sentiment analysis. It's like having a personal assistant in every meeting.

Dark Mode

You asked, we listened. Resonote now features a beautiful, system-wide dark mode that's easy on the eyes during those late-night editing sessions.

Improved Accuracy

We've integrated the latest Whisper models to provide near-human accuracy, even in noisy environments or with difficult accents.

Resonote 2.0 is available today for all users. Update your app or log in to the web dashboard to experience the future of transcription.
