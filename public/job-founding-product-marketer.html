Founding Product Marketer

Champaign, IL / Remote

About the Role

We are looking for a Founding Product Marketer to define the voice of Resonote and bring our AI-powered transcription tools to creators worldwide. You will be the first marketing hire, working directly with the founders to shape our brand, messaging, and go-to-market strategy.

What You'll Do

  • Craft compelling messaging and positioning for Resonote's features (Live Transcription, AI Insights, Background Recording).
  • Lead product launches and create marketing assets (demos, blog posts, landing pages).
  • Engage with our community of users to understand their needs and gather feedback.
  • Experiment with growth channels to drive user acquisition.

Requirements

  • 3+ years of product marketing experience, preferably in SaaS or AI.
  • Strong storytelling and copywriting skills.
  • Ability to translate technical features into user benefits.
  • Self-starter who thrives in a fast-paced, early-stage environment.
