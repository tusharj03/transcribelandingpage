Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Resonote

What audio and video formats are supported?

We support a wide range of formats including MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC, OGG, AAC, MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI, WebM, and more. If you have a specific format not listed, contact our support team.

How accurate is the transcription?

Our AI-powered transcription achieves over 95% accuracy for clear audio with a single speaker. Accuracy may vary with background noise, multiple speakers, or strong accents. All transcriptions can be easily edited within the app.

Can I use it for live transcription?

Yes! Resonote includes live audio recording and real-time transcription features. You can record meetings, interviews, or lectures and see the text appear as people speak.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We take data security seriously. All files are processed locally on your device when possible, and any cloud processing uses encrypted connections. We never share or sell your data.

What languages are supported?

Currently, we support English with high accuracy. Additional languages including Spanish, French, German, and Chinese are in beta testing and will be available soon.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access to all features until the end of your billing period.
